Take Another Quiz

Background

The purpose of this web app is to allow you to explore the past and current trends in computer programming interests. By browsing through a number of questions asked by an international group of programming learners and professionals, one can get a feel of the direction or trends of interest in computer programming languages and OSs.


Stack Overflow's website is a good location for exploring such trends. "It features questions and answers on a wide range of topics in computer programming. The website serves as a platform for users to ask and answer questions....As of April 2014, Stack Overflow has over 2,700,000 registered users and more than 7,100,000 questions." (Wipedia, Stack Overflow)


This App, in a fairly cursory but useful way, allows you to test your knowledge about the trend of a diverse, and technical computer programming audience. This knowledge, in turn, could, perhaps, in a small way, help you to make carreer related decisions.


You could explore the current and past trends in programming by first taking the 4-question quiz (click Take Quiz tab), then explore the answers by looking at the trend data under the Interest Trend tab. (Currntly, only one quiz in pool; this is expected to increase.)
